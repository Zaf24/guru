About us

Join us at Guru as we transform the way education is delivered, building stronger connections and fostering better outcomes for students, parents, and tutors.

Mission Statement

To revolutionise the tuition industry by creating a transparent, accessible, and AI-driven education platform that fosters meaningful connections between parents, students, and tutors, empowering every learner to achieve their full potential.

Redefining the Tuition Experience

Our app integrates innovative features inspired by leading platforms in other sectors, creating a user-friendly and efficient space for both parents and tutors. From removing price bargaining to offering an intuitive platform for hosting lessons, we’re committed to making tuition focus on what it's meant for, helping the student improve their academia.

Vision Statement

To become the leading education platform in Singapore and beyond, recognized for providing unparalleled features like AI-powered question scanning, curated notes, interactive videos, and a comprehensive learning ecosystem utilised by every student.

Features

Built-in Video Conferencing

Say goodbye to costly third-party video subscriptions. Guru includes built-in video and audio calling, screen sharing, and a virtual whiteboard, creating a seamless virtual classroom experience without extra charges.

Built-in Chat System

Communication is key to successful learning. Our integrated chat system keeps tutors, parents, and students connected, enabling instant messaging for lesson updates, queries, and feedback.

Personalized Progress Tracking

Stay on top of your learning journey with personalized progress reports. Tutors can set milestones, track performance, and share detailed insights with parents to ensure continuous improvement.

Regular Notifications & Reminders

Never miss a lesson or deadline again! Automated notifications keep everyone informed about upcoming classes, assignments, and important platform updates.

Secure Payment Processing

Managing payments has never been easier. Our secure, built-in payment system ensures fast, hassle-free transactions with full transparency for both parents and tutors.
